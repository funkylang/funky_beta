std::horizontal_group

creates a horizontal group of widgets

Parameters

widgets
the widgets to group
TOP
align the widgets at the top
BOTTOM
align the widgets at the bottom
MIDDLE
align the widgets in the middle

Result

group
the group of widgets

Description

The widgets are aligned vertically according to the specified alignment.

Topic

Widgets

See also

std::vertical_group creates a vertical group of widgets
std::table_group creates a table group

Example

$dialog create_dialog(wm "My Dialog")
  horizontal_group
    TOP
    text_box("This is a text box")
    push_button("This is a push button")
