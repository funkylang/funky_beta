std_types::view/std::invert

inverts the colours of the specified rectangular area within the view

Parameters

Description

view: the view to invert

screen: the screen to invert on

x: x-coordinate of the upper left corner (relative to the view)

y: y-coordinate of the upper left corner (relative to the view)

width: width of the area

height: height of the area

Result

screen
the updated screen

The specified area is inverted.

The area is clipped at the view's borders.

Terminal
