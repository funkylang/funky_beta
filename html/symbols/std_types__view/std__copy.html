std_types::view/std::copy

Copies a rectangular area from one screen to another.

Parameters

Description

view: the view to copy to destination: the destination screen source: the source screen x: x-coordinate of the upper left corner in the destination view y: y-coordinate of the upper left corner in the destination view sx: x-coordinate of the upper left corner in the source screen sy: y-coordinate of the upper left corner in the source screen width: width of the area to copy height: height of the area to copy

Result

destination
the destination screen

Copies a rectangular area from the source screen to the destination view.

The area is clipped at the borders of both the source screen and the destination view.
