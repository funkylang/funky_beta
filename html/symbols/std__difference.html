std::difference

computes the difference of two collections

Parameters

self
the base collection
other
the collection of items to remove

Result

difference
the difference of the two collections

Description

The difference of two collections is a collection containing all the items of the first collection that are not contained in the second collection.

Topic

Collections

See also

std::intersection compute the intersection of two collections
std::merge merges two collections

Example

$a create_list(1 2 3 4 5)
$b create_list(3 4 5 6 7)
dump! difference(&a &b)

Output

list <2>
  1
  2
