std::move

moves a rectangular area from one position to another within a screen

Parameters

screen # the screen to move the area from
x
x-coordinate of the destination
y
y-coordinate of the destination
sx
x-coordinate of the source
sy
y-coordinate of the source
width
width of the area
height
height of the area

Result

screen
the updated screen

Description

The area is moved from the source position (sx, sy) to the destination position (x, y).

Topic

Terminal

See also

std::copy copies a rectangulay area from one screen to another
std::draw_text draws text on a screen

Example

$my_screen create_screen(terminal 40 25) set_clear_colour &my_screen GREEN draw_text &my_screen 10 10 "Hello, world!" move &my_screen 15 15 10 10 10 5 update_terminal &my_screen $output # create output character stream print! output # print the generated character stream
