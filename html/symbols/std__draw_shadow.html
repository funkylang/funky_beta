std::draw_shadow

draws a shadow

Parameters

screen
some kind of screen
x1
x-coordinate of the upper left corner
y1
y-coordinate of the upper left corner
x2
x-coordinate of the lower right corner
y2
y-coordinate of the lower right corner

Result

screen
the updated screen

Description

The shadow is drawn using the current draw colour. Use std::set_draw_colour to change the current draw colour.

Topic

Terminal

See also

std_types::screen/std::draw_shadow draws a shadow around a rectangular area
std::draw_border draws a border around a rectangular area
std::draw_thick_border draws a thick border around a rectangular area
std::set_draw_colour sets the draw colour

Example

$my_screen create_screen(terminal 40 25) set_draw_colour &my_screen RED draw_shadow &my_screen 11 6 20 15
