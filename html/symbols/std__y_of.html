std::y_of

the y-coordinate of the upper left corner

Parameter

self
the object from which to get the y-coordinate

Result

y
the y-coordinate

Description

Returns the y-coordinate of the upper left corner of the object.

Topic

Terminal

See also

std::x_of the x-coordinate of the upper left corner

Example

$my_screen create_screen(terminal 40 25)
println! "The y-coordinate of the screen is: " y_of(&my_screen)
