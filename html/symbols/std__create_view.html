std::create_view

creates a view on a terminal

Parameters

screen
the terminal
left_x
the left x-coordinate of the view
top_y
the top y-coordinate of the view
right_x
the right x-coordinate of the view
bottom_y
the bottom y-coordinate of the view

Result

view
the view

Description

The view is a rectangular area on the terminal.

Topic

Terminal

See also

std::create_vertical_panes creates a list of views that are arranged vertically
std::create_horizontal_panes creates a number of horizontal panes

Example

$screen create_terminal() $view create_view(screen 2 2 10 5) dump! 2 `view
