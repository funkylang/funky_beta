std::copy

copies a rectangulay area from one screen to another

Parameters

destination
the destination screen
source
the source screen
x
x-coordinate of the upper left corner of the destination area
y
y-coordinate of the upper left corner of the destination area
sx
x-coordinate of the upper left corner of the source area
sy
y-coordinate of the upper left corner of the source area
width
width of the area to copy
height
height of the area to copy

Result

destination
the updated destination screen

Description

The area is copied from the source screen to the destination screen.

The area is clipped at the source and destination screen's borders.

Topic

Terminal

Example

$my_screen create_screen(terminal 40 25) set_clear_colour &my_screen GREEN clear &my_screen 11 6 20 15 $source_screen create_screen(terminal 40 25) draw_text &source_screen 10 5 "Hello, world!" copy &my_screen &source_screen 11 6 10 5 20 1
