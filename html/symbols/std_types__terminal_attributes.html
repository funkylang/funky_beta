Example

"restart output on any character: " !attributes restart_output_on_any_character println! "ignore cr on input: " !attributes ignore_cr_on_input println! "generate signals: " !attributes generate_signals println! "map cr to lf on input: " !attributes map_cr_to_lf_on_input println! "hangup on close: " !attributes hangup_on_close println!
