ai::pieces_of

returns a list of all pieces (tokens) known to the AI model.

Parameter

self
the AI model

Result

pieces
a list of strings

Example

$modelname "sauerkrautem-una-solar-instruc.Q5_K_M.gguf" $prompt "Once upon a time" ai::load_ai_model! $model modelname ai::pieces_of! $pieces model print! pieces

["Once", "upon", "a", "time"]
