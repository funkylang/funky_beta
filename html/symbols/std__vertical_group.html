std::vertical_group

creates a vertical group of widgets

Parameters

widgets
the widgets to group
LEFT
align widgets to the left (default)
RIGHT
align widgets to the right
CENTER
align widgets to the center

Result

group
the group of widgets

Description

The widgets are arranged vertically.

Topic

Widgets

See also

std::horizontal_group creates a horizontal group of widgets
std::table_group creates a table group

Example

$dialog create_dialog(wm "My Dialog")
  vertical_group
    CENTER
    text_box("This is a dialog")
    push_button("Ok" = OKAY)
