std::colour_mixture

mixes multiple colours into one

Parameters

args (zero or more)
the colours to mix, each possibly with a weight

Result

a new colour that is the mixture of the given colours

Description

Each colour can be specified with a weight (e.g. `colour=weight`), which determines how much of that colour is used in the mixture.

If no weight is given, the default weight is 1.

The resulting colour is the average of all the given colours, weighted by their respective weights.
