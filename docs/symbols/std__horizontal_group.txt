# std::horizontal_group (a function)

  creates a horizontal group of widgets

  Parameters:
    widgets: the widgets to group
    TOP: align the widgets at the top
    BOTTOM: align the widgets at the bottom
    MIDDLE: align the widgets in the middle

  Result:
    group: the group of widgets

  The widgets are aligned vertically according to the specified alignment.

  Topic: Widgets

  See also: std::vertical_group, std::table_group

  Example:

    $dialog create_dialog(wm "My Dialog")
      horizontal_group
        TOP
        text_box("This is a text box")
        push_button("This is a push button")
