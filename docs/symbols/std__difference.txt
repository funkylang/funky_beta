# std::difference (a polymorphic function)

  computes the difference of two collections

  Parameters:
    self: the base collection
    other: the collection of items to remove

  Result:
    difference: the difference of the two collections

  The difference of two collections is a collection containing all the items
  of the first collection that are not contained in the second collection.

  Topic: Collections

  See also: std::intersection, std::merge

  Example:

    $a create_list(1 2 3 4 5)
    $b create_list(3 4 5 6 7)
    dump! difference(&a &b)

  Output:

    list <2>
      1
      2
