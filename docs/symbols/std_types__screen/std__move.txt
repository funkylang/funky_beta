# std_types::screen/std::move (a method)

  moves a rectangular area of the screen

  Parameters:
    screen: the screen to be modified
    x: x-coordinate of the upper left corner of the destination area
    y: y-coordinate of the upper left corner of the destination area
    sx: x-coordinate of the upper left corner of the source area
    sy: y-coordinate of the upper left corner of the source area
    width: width of the area
    height: height of the area

  Result:
    screen: the updated screen

  The specified area is moved to the specified destination.

  The source area is filled with spaces using the the current draw-
  and clear colour.

  The source and destination areas are clipped at the screen's borders.

  This function also works for "graphical screens" (line_height != 1).

  Topic: Terminal

  See also: std_types::screen/std::copy
