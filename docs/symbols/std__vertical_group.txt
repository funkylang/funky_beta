# std::vertical_group (a function)

  creates a vertical group of widgets

  Parameters:
    widgets: the widgets to group
    LEFT: align widgets to the left (default)
    RIGHT: align widgets to the right
    CENTER: align widgets to the center

  Result:
    group: the group of widgets

  The widgets are arranged vertically.

  Topic: Widgets

  See also: std::horizontal_group, std::table_group

  Example:

    $dialog create_dialog(wm "My Dialog")
      vertical_group
        CENTER
        text_box("This is a dialog")
        push_button("Ok" = OKAY)
