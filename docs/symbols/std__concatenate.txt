# std::concatenate (a function)

  concatenates two or more lists

  Parameters:
    separator: the separator to insert between the lists

  Result:
    the concatenated list

  Topic: Lists

  See also: std::concatenate_non_empty

  Example:

    $list1 list(1 2 3)
    $list2 list(4 5 6)
    $list3 list(7 8 9)
    $list4 concatenate(list1 list2 list3)
    dump! 2 `list4

  Output:

    list4:
      list
        1
        2
        3
        4
        5
        6
        7
        8
        9
