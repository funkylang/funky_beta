# ai::pieces_of (a polymorphic function)

  returns a list of all pieces (tokens) known to the AI model.

  Parameter:
    self: the AI model

  Result:
    pieces: a list of strings

  Example:

    $modelname "sauerkrautem-una-solar-instruc.Q5_K_M.gguf"
    $prompt "Once upon a time"
    ai::load_ai_model! $model modelname
    ai::pieces_of! $pieces model
    print! pieces

  Output:

    ["Once", "upon", "a", "time"]
