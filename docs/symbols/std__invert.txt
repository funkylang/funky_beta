# std::invert (a polymorphic function)

  inverts a rectangular area

  Parameters:
    screen: some kind of screen
    x: x-coordinate of the upper left corner
    y: y-coordinate of the upper left corner
    width: width of the area
    height: height of the area

  Result:
    screen: the updated screen

  The area is inverted using the current draw colour. Use
  <std::set_draw_colour> to change the current draw colour.

  The area is clipped at the screen's borders.

  Topic: Terminal

  See also: std_types::screen/std::invert, std_types::view/std::invert,
            std::draw_text, std::set_draw_colour

  Example:

    $my_screen create_screen(terminal 40 25)
    set_draw_colour &my_screen GREEN
    invert &my_screen 11 6 20 15
