# std::draw_thick_border (a polymorphic function)

  draws a thick border around a rectangular area

  Parameters:
    screen: some kind of screen
    x1: x-coordinate of the upper left corner
    y1: y-coordinate of the upper left corner
    x2: x-coordinate of the lower right corner
    y2: y-coordinate of the lower right corner

  Result:
    screen: the updated screen

  The border is drawn using the current draw colour. Use
  <std::set_draw_colour> to change the current draw colour.

  The border is clipped at the screen's borders.

  Topic: Terminal

  See also: std_types::screen/std::draw_thick_border, std::draw_border,
            std::draw_text, std::set_draw_colour

  Example:

    $my_screen create_screen(terminal 40 25)
    set_draw_colour &my_screen RED
    draw_thick_border &my_screen 11 6 20 15
